What's inside

Everything you need,
right when you need it

Designed for the operating theatre environment — fast to open, fast to navigate, no login required.

💊
Drug Dosing Calculator
Weight-based induction and maintenance doses for 40+ anaesthetic agents. Adult and paediatric modes. Ceiling dose warnings.
40+ agents
🫁
Airway Algorithm
DAS and DGAI difficult airway decision trees. Can't intubate / can't oxygenate pathways. Equipment checklists.
DAS / DGAI
🚨
Crisis Checklists
Step-by-step emergency protocols for MH, anaphylaxis, LAST, perioperative cardiac arrest, high spinal and equipment failure.
6 emergencies
🗺️
Regional Anaesthesia Atlas
20+ nerve blocks with ultrasound views, anatomical landmarks, drug dosing, depth, mechanism and risk profiles.
20+ blocks
📊
Preoperative Risk Scoring
ASA classification, cardiac risk (RCRI/Lee), Mallampati, Apfel PONV score and airway predictors in one place.
RCRI · Apfel · ASA
🧪
Fluid Calculator
Perioperative fluid strategy, maintenance rates, deficit estimation and resuscitation targets by weight and clinical context.
Holliday-Segar
✅
Procedure Checklists
WHO surgical safety checklist, regional, obstetric and paediatric protocols. Tap to check off each step.
Interactive

Drug doses and protocols aligned with current German anaesthesia society guidelines and Fachinformation references.
Germany-ready

The developer

Built by a real anaesthetist, for real anaesthetists.

I'm an MD and anaesthesiologist — London-born, trained in Bulgaria, now working in Germany. I built AnaesthesiaPro because I couldn't find a clinical reference app that matched how I actually think in theatre: fast, offline-first, and aligned with German guidelines.

Every drug dose, every crisis checklist, and every nerve block description has been written and reviewed by me personally. This is the app I wish I'd had as a registrar.

MD Anaesthesiologist London-born Bulgaria-trained Germany DGAI/AWMF
